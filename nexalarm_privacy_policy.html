Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

NexAlarm (referred to as "the App" or "the Developer") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the NexAlarm mobile application.

1. Information Collection and Use

NexAlarm is designed to respect your privacy. The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal identification information. All your alarm settings and data are stored locally on your device.

2. Permissions

To provide its core functionality, NexAlarm requires the following permissions:

  • Exact Alarm: Used to trigger alarms at the precise time you set.
  • Accessibility Service: This is an optional feature used as a "Protection Guard" to prevent accidental dismissal of alarms by users who struggle to wake up. No data is collected or shared through this service.
  • Device Administrator: Used to prevent the app from being uninstalled while an alarm is active.
  • Notifications: Used to show alarm alerts and reminders.

3. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's security settings.

4. Third-Party Services

NexAlarm does not use any third-party analytics or advertising services that track your behavior.

5. Changes to This Policy

The Developer may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
